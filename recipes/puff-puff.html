Puff-puff Recipe

A bowl of delicious puff-puff

Puff-puff, a decadent deep-fried dough which is inexpensive and easy to make. This recipe is easy to follow and will have whipping up a delicious snack for you and family or friends to enjoy

Ingredients
  • 500g of Plain Flour
  • 100g of Sugar
  • 15g of Dried Yeast
  • 5g Salt
  • 10ml of Vanilla Paste
  • 300ml of Water
  • 150ml of Lukewarm Water
  • 2l of cooking oil
Method
  1. Transfer the lukewarm water into a bowl and dissolve 20g of the sugar in it.
  2. Add the yeast to the sugar solution and whisk the mixture rigorously.
  3. Cover with the mixture and set aside in a warm place for 10-15 minutes or until frothy.
  4. Transfer the flour, the salt and the rest of the sugar in a large and mix well.
  5. Once the yeast mixture is frothy, whisk again to dissolve and transfer it to the flour mixture.
  6. Mix in the water until a smooth stretchy runny dough is achieved.
  7. Cover with tablecloth and let it proof for 2 hours or until double in size.
  8. Transfer the cooking oil into a wok or deep pot over medium heat.
  9. Let the oil heat up to 170C.
  10. Get two soup spoons and scoop the dough with one and use the other to transfer the dough into the hot oil
  11. Repeat this until the wok or pot has ran out of cooking surface.
  12. Fry until golden and transfer the puff-puff unto a kitchen towel.
  13. Repeat until all dough has been fried.
  14. Enjoy.
